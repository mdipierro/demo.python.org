Minutes of a Regular Meeting of the Board of Directors

February 8, 2010

A regular meeting of the Python Software Foundation ("PSF") Board of Directors was held over Internet Relay Chat beginning at 17:00 UTC, 8 February 2010. Steve Holden presided at the meeting. Pat Campbell prepared the minutes.

All votes are reported in the form "Y-N-A" (in favor ? opposed ? abstentions; e.g. "5-1-2" means "5 in favor, 1 opposed, and 2 abstentions").

9 Copyright Assignment Current Status & Next Step

The Board discussed the extent of copyright coverage and agreed that 100% coverage should be sought by the PSF.

A. Kuchling: "Today I re-ran the 'svn blame' for the current 2.7 and 3.2 branches. 2.7 has only 8.5% not covered and 3.2 has 9.9% not covered.

So, what next? Analyze which files aren't covered? Add an assignment flag to PyPI? I've started on a patch for that. Put this flag in MatrixMaxx? I'd like to see this task through, even though I won't be on the board."

  1. Holden: "Thank you for that."
  2. Peters: "100% coverage is the goal, right?"

S. Holden: "I think we probably want a continual push to increase coverage."

  1. Peters: "The next step is to get closer to 100%."
